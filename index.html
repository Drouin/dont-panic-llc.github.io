WHO WE ARE

Welcome to our temporary site. This is up while we are building our full site.

With our roots in consulting services and developing advanced blockchain technologies, Don’t Panic has evolved to become primarily a software development firm, leveraging our experience with secure advanced technologies, and customer experience to shape better and more integrated software. Don’t Panic believes that complex, cutting edge technology usually reserved for large organizations, should be available to all organizations at a reasonable price.

What We Do

Consultancy Services

We provide various software, management, and marketing consultancy services for small and medium sized businesses.

Software Services

Don’t Panic will still provide consulting services to select clients, but our primary focus is now on building a business management and customer relationship management platform, codenamed Eden, specifically for small and medium sized businesses. Initially focusing on New England ski resorts, we hope to provide world class service at a fraction of the cost of our larger competitors.

If you are one of the small or medium sized businesses that do not have the time, financial resources, or expertise to make the essential data-based decisions necessary for sustainable growth, Don’t Panic has the solution for you! - The Eden Business Suite enables any business to perform all of the critical customer facing, and back office software functions using only one system, instead of using the fragmented business solutions that are currently available.

The Eden Business Suite, includes: customer relationship management software, marketing automation, sales automation, invoicing, inventory management, contracting, procurement, business analytics, and marketing tools for all businesses

Problems we are trying to solve

Complicated business systems
Business tools are usually complicated, and difficult to maintain, requiring teams of administrators and developers to tailor to each business. For small businesses, this is a huge drain on tight resources.

Expensive
Business tools are expensive to deploy, costing thousands to millions of dollars per year to maintain and store data in. This is prohibitive to small businesses, and painful for medium businesses to handle.

Too Many Tools
Many tools are needed to conduct business; Enterprise Resource Planning, Customer Relationship Management, Case Management, Knowledge Base, Accounting, Inventory, all with their own price tags.

Fragmented business tools
No company currently builds in all of the business tools for CRM, ERP, and data-driven decision making.

Our Solutions

Unified Business Tools
Instead of 3+ tools to run a business, just use the Eden Business Management Suite - One tool for all of your business needs to help you sell, manage, and grow.

Easy to Use, Easy to Manage
Ease of use is critical to business user adoption. Ease of management is critical for business administrators peace of mind. No 1AM phone calls, and no complexity in user interface.

Customizable
Eden is fully customizable to your business and ensures all your sales-staff collect the information you need on every lead. Ensures your support staff properly document cases.

Data Analytics
Data is collected and returned to each user, providing industry sector trends and insight tools for each individual business to make data-driven decisions based on their unique needs.
